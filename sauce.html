Chef's Secrets

Heating & Serving

Never Boil

Heat the sauce gently over medium-low heat. Boiling ruins the delicate flavors of the olive oil and fresh herbs.

Add Pasta Water

Stir in a splash of starchy pasta water into the simmering sauce to help bind it beautifully to the noodles.

Toss in the Pan

Never pour sauce over naked pasta on a plate. Toss the pasta directly in the pan with the sauce for one minute.

Garnish Generously

Finish off heat with a drizzle of fresh olive oil, torn basil leaves, and freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano.
