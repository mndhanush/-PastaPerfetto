Knowledge Base

How can we help?

Category 1

Shipping & Delivery

We pack all of our fresh pastas and sauces inside eco-friendly, insulated liners along with non-toxic gel ice packs. This ensures your food stays at safe, refrigerated temperatures for up to 48 hours during transit.

Shipping is a flat rate of $9.99 for all standard A La Carte orders. However, shipping is completely free for all Pasta Club and La Famiglia members, as well as on any order over $75.

Currently, we only ship within the contiguous United States. Due to the highly perishable nature of our fresh ingredients, we cannot guarantee freshness on international or overseas routes at this time.

Category 2

Product & Quality

Our fresh pasta contains no preservatives. Once received, it will stay fresh in your refrigerator for up to 5 days. If you don't plan to eat it right away, you can freeze the pasta in its original packaging for up to 3 months without losing quality.

We are currently developing an artisanal gluten-free pasta line that meets our high standards for texture and chew. At this moment, all of our pastas are made with traditional durum wheat semolina and are not safe for those with Celiac disease.

Yes. We source our heirloom wheat, farm eggs, tomatoes, and herbs exclusively from certified organic farms in Italy and local organic purveyors. We believe chemical-free soil creates the best tasting food.

Category 3

Memberships & Billing

When you join the Pasta Club, you'll be billed monthly. Each month, we curate a box of seasonal pastas and perfect pairing sauces. You'll get an email 3 days before shipping allowing you to swap out any items, or keep the Chef's selection.

Not at all. We hate hidden cancellation buttons. You can pause, skip a delivery, or cancel your subscription entirely with one click inside your user dashboard. No calling customer service required.

Our La Famiglia members get exclusive access to our highly limited, small-batch runs. This includes rare ingredients like Alba white truffles, aged balsamic vinegars, and highly specialized, intricate pasta shapes that we don't produce for the general store.

Dedicated Support

Can't find what you're looking for? Our dedicated team of culinary experts and support staff are always ready to help you craft the perfect meal.

General Inquiries

For non-culinary questions, order tracking, or wholesale requests, our customer service team is ready to assist you.
