The Story Behind the Semolina

Rooted in
Tradition.

What started as a small family kitchen in Sicily has blossomed into a passion project to bring authentic, uncompromised pasta to the world. We believe that good food shouldn't be complicated, but it should be crafted with absolute devotion.

"Pasta is not just food. It is history, geography, and love served on a plate."

Our History

Our Journey

1985

The First Knead

Nonna Rosa opened our first small shop, rolling out fresh sheets of egg pasta by hand every single morning for the local neighborhood.

2001

The Bronze Die Era

We acquired our first traditional bronze-die extruders, allowing us to create intricate shapes that hold sauce perfectly without losing the artisanal touch.

Today

To Your Table

With a commitment to 100% organic ingredients and eco-friendly packaging, we now deliver the true taste of Italy nationwide.

How It's Made

The Crafting Process

01

Sourcing the Grain

We exclusively use heirloom durum wheat sourced from organic, sustainable farms in southern Italy. It provides the high protein content needed for perfect al dente pasta.

02

Milling & Mixing

The wheat is cold-milled to preserve its nutrients and flavor profile. We mix it slowly with pure spring water to gently develop the gluten network without heating the dough.

03

Extrusion & Slow Drying

Our dough is pushed through vintage bronze dies, giving the pasta a rough, porous surface that grips sauces tightly. It is then air-dried at low temperatures for 48 hours to lock in the flavor.
