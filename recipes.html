Chef's Secrets

Rules of the Kitchen

Salty Like the Sea

Pasta water should be heavily salted. It's your only chance to season the pasta itself before it meets the sauce.

Never Rinse

Rinsing cooked pasta washes away the precious starches needed to help the sauce cling to the noodles.

Liquid Gold

Always save a cup of starchy pasta water before draining. Use it to thin out and emulsify your sauces.

Finish in the Pan

Drain pasta 1 minute early and finish cooking it directly in the simmering sauce for maximum flavor absorption.
